What is in Munch for me?

Munch is currently an early stage startup with the mission of making a significant change on the current state of the growing Online Deals Industry. We are developing a platform that makes the Online Food Deals Advertisement that enhances the experience for customers and restaurant owners by giving full control over their experience. By joining us as an investor at this stage you will be at a position to share our journey and enjoy maximum benefits from your investment.

What is in Munch for restaurant owners?

Other platform main channel of revenue is taking a comission fee between 30% and 50%, restaurant owners go with this route because it is the only option but the main competitors in the Industry have been reported to have client disatisfaction of above 50%. Munch is focusing in creating the best experience to restaurant owners by helping them track and modify their offers on real time. Additionally, we will charge restaurant an upfront fee for posting their deals so that they can keep all of the income produced by their sales.

What is in Munch for the users?

Users will recieve deals location based or by filtering restaurants that they like the most. This way, we prevent a flood of unnecesary advertisement while providing the best experience when looking for a deal.
